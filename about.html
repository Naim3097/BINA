Our story

A small studio with a clear conviction: design and build belong together.

BINA+ began as a side venture inside Boxup — a Malaysian product studio — when our team kept watching homeowners get caught between architects who wouldn't build, and contractors who wouldn't design. So we built the in-between.

Why we exist

Two years in, 85 builds delivered, one philosophy.

We believe the best homes happen when the person drawing the plans is the same team standing on the slab. That's why every BINA+ project moves through one studio, one project manager, and one fixed contract.

Our studio is led by Syafiq (principal) — Selangor-born and stubborn about quality. We work on a deliberately small slate of projects each year so every build gets the attention it deserves.

BINA+ is registered as a separate SSM entity from our parent brand Boxup, but we share the same operations DNA: clear scopes, weekly updates, and zero "you'll see when it's done" surprises.

What we stand for

Three rules we don't break.

01 · Pricing

Fixed scope, fixed price

Every quote is line-itemised. If we missed something, that's our cost — not yours. Variation orders only happen when you ask for them.

02 · Climate

Built for the climate

Designed for Malaysian heat, humidity and the monsoon. Cross-ventilation, deep eaves and proper waterproofing aren't upgrades here — they're defaults.

03 · People

One team, all the way

Same project manager from day one to handover. No subcontractor finger-pointing, no "that's not our scope." Accountability is built in.
