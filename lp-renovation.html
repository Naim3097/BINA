Common questions

Things people ask before they sign

How long does a typical reno take?

Renovation: 6 to 12 weeks. Single storey design and build: 14 to 20 weeks. Double storey: 20 to 28 weeks. The exact handover date is written into your contract.

Do you handle JMB or strata renovation forms?

Yes for all strata properties. We submit the renovation form, follow up with the management office, and only start demolition once the approval letter is in hand.

What if costs blow out mid build?

All variations are itemised the same way as the original quote. Anything above 10% of contract value triggers a written re baseline. No verbal "tambah sikit".

Can I supply my own materials?

Yes for finishes such as tiles, taps, lights, paint and wallpaper. No for structural items such as cement, steel and electrical wiring. We carry the warranty on those, so we control the source.

What is your warranty?

12 months on workmanship from the handover date. Manufacturer warranty pass through on installed products such as pumps, smart locks and fittings.
